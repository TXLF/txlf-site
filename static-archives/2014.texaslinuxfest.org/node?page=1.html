Happy 2014!

Submitted by AndyJames on Mon, 01/06/2014 - 3:54pm

Happy New Year! We are so excited that Texas Linux Fest 2014 is only 6 months from now.

We are busy planning, working with our sponsors, and coming up with great new ideas to make this years festival even more exciting!

Have you got an idea that you think we may like? Reach out and let us know! You can contact us here. If you would like to volunteer at this years conference, visit the volunteer page.

As always if you would like to speak, just visit the CFP page. We are always looking for new and interesting topics.

Last but not least, make sure you get registered!

We wanted to leave you with the video from 2013 as a reminder of all the excitement last year! Of course if you need more, there is lots of pictures too! Cant wait to see you this year!

Texas Linux Fest 2014!

Submitted by AndyJames on Mon, 10/28/2013 - 5:29pm
