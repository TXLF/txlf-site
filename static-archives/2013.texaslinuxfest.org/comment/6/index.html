Call For Papers extended until Friday April 5

Submitted by nwillis on Mon, 04/01/2013 - 12:30pm

Good news! Due to requests from our speakers and vendors about the holiday weekend, we have decided to push back the 2013 Call For Papers until Friday, April 5, at 11:59 pm, Central Time.

The rest of the schedule remains unchanges; we will contact all speakers by April 15th. We hope to begin notifying speakers about talk selections this week, but if you have already submitted a talk, don't worry. We have received a lot of proposals already, sorting through them is never a simple task!

You can read the full details about the 2013 program and the talk submission process at the CFP page: http://2013.texaslinuxfest.org/cfp
