Texas Linux Fest 2015 - keynote https://2015.texaslinuxfest.org/tags/keynote en Apache's Joan Touzet to give keynote https://2015.texaslinuxfest.org/content/apaches-joan-touzet-give-keynote <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>Joan Touzet of the Apache Software Foundation has been chosen as the keynote speaker for this year's Texas Linux Fest.</p> <p>Joan will speak on the topic "Evolve or Perish! Improving OSS Communities The Apache Way." </p> <p>From the summary, we get a good idea of what to expect: "Change is an inevitable part of growing an open source community. In this talk, learn how one OSS community (Apache CouchDB) reinvigorated itself from a series of seemingly insurmountable challenges. With lessons learned from video gaming, theatre and music, CouchDB rediscovered The Apache Way and turned a toxic environment into a positive, supporting place for positive change."</p> <p>Texas Linux Fest runs from Aug. 21-23 in San Marcos, Texas.
